About Me

Now that you've answered my questions, here are a few related details:

  • I am 6'4", which is tall enough that people often comment on it, like I had no idea. Though, to be fair, sometimes I forget myself when I reach for something and realize that my arms extend out as far as they do.
  • I was born in Washington State, and if I have my way, I'll never leave permanently. We're all spoiled by the grandeur of Mother Nature here, and I try not to take it for granted.
  • Gloomy weather can have its effect on me, but for the most part I actually enjoy the rain. Falling asleep to the sound of it is very soothing, and a reminder that even when it's miserable outside, there are still benefits (in addition to keeping everything so green!)
  • I've always enjoyed going to Seahawks games, but I didn't become a serious fan until after the team's first Super Bowl run in 2005/2006. I watched the playoffs with my roommates at the time, and really enjoyed the experience, so I decided to watch the regular season games, too. Because of this, I find the idea of "fair weather" fans to be kind of silly. We're all fair weather fans until we're not!
  • While I've always lived in Washington, I've moved around quite a bit, spending elementary school in Federal Way, junior high and high school in Sumner, and college up in Bellingham, and that's far from a comprehensive list of everywhere I've lived. In fact, I've moved so often that whenever anyone I know asks for help moving, I'm always up for it, because it's something I've done so much of myself (and I've always appreciated the help I've received!)

As far as my background goes, after high school, I went to Western Washington University, where I got a bachelor's degree in general studies. Since then, I've worked at my alma mater as a transcriber and at Apple Retail. I'm looking forward to what's next as I embark on a career of coding!
